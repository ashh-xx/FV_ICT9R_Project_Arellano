About Us

Safety 101 was a website made to ensure that everyone gets through their daily lives safely and happily. We provide informative articles on how to stay safe when different catastrophies occur like earthquakes and storms, or when you are come face-to-face with something you might cause like electrical mishaps and the highlight of this month, Fire.




It was challenging to get to where we are now and to make this project successful but with the immense support we had gotten since the start, we have now reached over a thousand people! It is refreshing and great to know that our team's efforts had gone to a great cause. From the creators of the website itself to the publishers who took it out of their time to thoroughly research for everyone else's safety.
