Fire Emergency Hotlines in the Philippines

Published March 20, 2023

Image Source: https://www.dexcomm.com/

In case of any accident or disaster, there will be somebody authorized that you can call for help. Same goes with fire. Here are the hotlines you may call when a fire breaks out.

Landline Hotline Numbers:

Department Contact Number
National Emergency Hotline 911
Quezon City Fire District 8330-2344
Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) (02) 8426-0219
(02) 8426-0246

It is important to take note of these numbers and other emergency hotlines to ensure that you and your loved ones are always safe. These contact numbers mighy vary from your area, but these are the most commonly used in Metro Manila and specifically Quezon City.
